What to expect

Hi there, Welcome to Cornell Tech! Your co-workers would love to know more about you, and in order to do that, we need to ask you a couple of questions. Some general personal information, you past experience and fun facts would allow us to generate a profile card for you. There are two additional pieces that we need you to prepare beforehand:

  • A photo of yourself as your user profile picture
  • Your favorite picture, which can or cannot include yourself. This picture will be displayed on your desk publicly, and it could be anything you like (your dog, a pokemon, etc)
